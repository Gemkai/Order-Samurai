## Why it's shaped this way
- Front-loads the **WIP-aware commit** rule and the **rival-model gotcha** (`claude-fable-5`
  inaccessible → override to sonnet) — the two things most likely to trip a fresh session.
- W2.2 carries a **`/subagent-audit` guardrail**: without it, "let ronins spawn specialists" would
  *add* to the 19/173 wasteful-spawn problem the governance-sweep already found.
- Includes the **async-wrapper `node --check`** trick, since plain `node --check` rejects the
  workflow runtime's top-level `return`/`await`.
